Welcome to Crave!

San Antonio's Premier Vegan and Vegetarian Mobile Kitchen

Our Story

Crave was started by a lifelong vegetarian and his family. We have many vegan and vegetarian friends as well. We are combining our knowledge and experience from our years of travel and eating vegan and vegetarian and are bringing our favorites to you!

Our Food

As vegetarians and vegans it can be hard to find options at most restaurants, with few menu choices. We have taken many of our favorite dishes from all over the world and brought them together for you to enjoy. We have samplings from all over the world for your dining pleasure, enjoy!
